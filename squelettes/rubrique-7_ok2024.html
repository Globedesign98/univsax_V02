The Université Européenne de Saxophone usually takes place in the second half of July, and lasts 12 days. Inscriptions will be taken into account until the 9th june 2024, subject to availability.

RATE : 940 €

Rate includes registration fees for teaching classes (500€), as well as housing and food (breakfast, lunch and dinner) at the Foyer des Jeunes Travailleurs in the city centre of Gap, during the duration of the University (440€).

RATE : 500 € (without housing)

RATE : 250 € (auditors)
